Card Game Rules

General Rules

Setup: Shuffle your deck and draw 5 cards to start. The player with the highest total attack damage goes first.

Deck Composition: Each deck must contain exactly 40 cards, including at least 5 attack cards and 5 defense cards.

Action Points: Each player gets 3 action points per round. Actions include playing, moving, or attacking with cards.

Win Condition

Each player starts with 3 hit points. Direct attacks reduce hit points, and the game ends when a player reaches 0 hit points.
